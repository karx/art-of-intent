How it works
  • Enter a cheat code as your prompt — exact or close enough (punctuation & case don't matter).
  • Arty writes you a playful tribute haiku that includes one of your remaining target words.
  • The word counts toward your win. No tokens consumed.
  • Your session is marked as a cheat session and will not appear on the daily leaderboard.
  • Each code can only gift one word per use — you may need to play fair for the others!
